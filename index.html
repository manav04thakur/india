India 1 ('Mexico')

The Consuming Class

Demographics

~10% of Population

~140m People

~$15k+ Per Capita Income

Population Share (Approx.)

  • Affluent, drives economic engine

Consumption Habits

Discretionary Spending Power (Approx. Share)

Relative Height Indicates Share

  • Accounts for ~2/3rds of discretionary spend
  • Consumes premium goods, services, experiences
  • Invests in equity, digital services
  • Prefers gated communities, global brands
  • Growth is 'deepening' (more premium) not 'widening'

Market Significance

  • Primary market for most startups (High ARPU)
  • Shapes premium market trends
  • Influences 'India0' (Diaspora) trends

India 2 ('Indonesia')

The Aspirant Class

Demographics

~20-25% of Population

~300m People

~$3k Per Capita Income

Population Share (Approx.)

  • Emerging, tech-savvy, value-conscious

Consumption Habits

Discretionary Spending Power (Approx. Share)

Relative Height Indicates Share

  • Accounts for ~1/3rd of discretionary spend
  • Relevant markets: OTT, Media, Gaming, EdTech, Lending
  • UPI/AutoPay unlocks small ticket spends
  • Requires adapted products (local context, lower price points)
  • Voice interaction and microtransactions are key

Market Significance

  • Large volume, but lower ticket size (Lower ARPU)
  • Requires specific 'India2 Playbooks'
  • Growing digital user base
  • Sensitive to pricing and local relevance

India 3 ('Sub-Saharan Africa')

The Next Billion / Unmonetisable Users

Demographics

~65-70% of Population

~1 Billion People

~$1k Per Capita Income

Population Share (Approx.)

  • Low disposable income

Consumption Habits

Discretionary Spending Power (Approx. Share)

Relative Height Indicates Share

  • Very limited discretionary spending
  • Focus on basic necessities
  • Basic mobile usage (e.g., WhatsApp, YouTube) may exist

Market Significance

  • Vast population, minimal current spending power for startups
  • Potential future market? Requires fundamental economic growth
  • Largely "unmonetised" or non-users currently
